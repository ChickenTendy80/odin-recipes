Caesar Salad

Description

The following recipe will take you through how to make a caesar salad from scratch, even the dressings!

Ingredients

Dressing
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 lemon juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
Salad
  • 1/2 head romaine lettuce,chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 table spoon croutons
